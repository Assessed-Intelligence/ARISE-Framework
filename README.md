## What ARISE Is

The ARISE Framework™ is a unified governance model that integrates cybersecurity, AI risk management, privacy, and ethics into one continuous assurance program. Organizations have been forced to navigate disparate frameworks and regulations with no single, unified approach; the foundational concepts across leading international standards are aligned, but the guidance is scattered. ARISE gathers it into one coherent, scalable program mapped across major global standards.

The framework is organized into seven domains:

| Domain | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| GOVERN | Strategy, accountability, oversight, and policy structures |
| MANAGE | Risk processes, lifecycle governance, and issue handling |
| IDENTIFY | Asset visibility, inventories, dependencies, and classification |
| PROTECT | Safeguards, access control, security, and privacy enforcement |
| DETECT | Monitoring, anomalies, control failures, and operational risk |
| RESPOND | Escalation, corrective action, incident handling, and recovery |
| VALIDATE | Testing, evidence, assurance, audit readiness, and independent review |

## What This Repository Contains

- `framework/` — the public control library: 128 controls across seven domains with all 889 prioritized requirements published in full, plus the 38-node taxonomy that organizes them
- `crosswalks/` — mappings between ARISE controls and 40 external frameworks, regulations, and audit criteria, including the EU AI Act, ISO/IEC 42001, ISO/IEC 27001, NIST AI RMF, NIST SP 800-53, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS v4.0, the Colorado AI Act, Texas TRAIGA, and the CEN/CENELEC prEN 18xxx series
- `docs/` — supporting documentation and methodology notes

Implementation rationale, evidence requirements, and the annotated framework mappings behind each control are maintained by Assessed Intelligence and available through engagements and commercial licensing.
